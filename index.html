About

I am a Ph.D. student (integrated M.S.–Ph.D. program) in the Department of Statistics at Seoul National University, and a member of the Causal AI Lab advised by Prof. Gunwoong Park. My research focuses on identifiable classes of structural causal models (SCMs) and on developing learning algorithms for them. Before turning to statistics, I studied life sciences at Konkuk University, where my interest in untangling cause and effect first took root.

Research

  • Identifiable classes of structural causal models (SCMs)
  • Learning algorithms for discrete and grouped SCMs
  • Outlier-robust causal discovery for high-dimensional linear structural equation models
